Membership Application

Please fill out the application below and submit your Santa Clara Tennis Club application. Membership runs January through December. Online payment is preferred using Paypal. Note that using Paypal does not require an account. After your membership is submitted the membership page will be updated with your membership.
For family memberships, a separate form must be completed for each additional family member. Each family member must have the same home address. When you fill out the application for the first family member, remember the home address that you use. In the online application, there is a $0.99 member charge for each additional family member. Fill out the form and follow the same procedure as for the primary family member.
Note that the club is required to have a Santa Clara resident majority of 50% + 1. Therefore at certain times of the year, membership will be restricted to Santa Clara residents.
